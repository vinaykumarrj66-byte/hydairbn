Frequently Asked Questions

Who pays the electricity and water bills?
All utility bills (Electricity, Water, WiFi) are deducted from the gross revenue. You receive the Net Profit directly. You don't have to worry about payment dates.
What if the property is vacant for some days?
Unlike traditional rent, Airbnb works on occupancy. However, our dynamic pricing team ensures that even with 70% occupancy, your total monthly income is significantly higher than regular rent.
Is it legal to run an Airbnb in Hyderabad?
Yes. We comply with all local regulations and maintain guest logs (Police Intimation/Form C where required) to ensure 100% legal compliance for your property.
Can I visit my property?
Absolutely. It's your property. We just ask for 24-hour notice to ensure we don't disturb a staying guest. You can also block dates for your personal use.
